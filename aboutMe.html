Background

Hey guys, My Name is Adham Oudeif. I am 21 years old and am a current Junior at EMU. I was born in Arizona, but was raised all over the world due to the nomadic lifestyle our family took on because of my Fathers job. I am from a great village in Yemen and am extremely proud of my roots. I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science. My family and culture are very tribal, and we uphold our roots and have pride so that the generations that come after us know who they are, and where they need to go. Currently my family resides here after coming from Yemen before I was born. Their intent was to create better lives for us, so that we may one day bring prosperity back to our homes.
